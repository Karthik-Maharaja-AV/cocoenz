This event is based on aptitude questions along with a fun session.It has 3 rounds.
1st round --
In this round quantitative based questions will be displayed in the mentimeter platform.Top 20 will be selected for the next round
2nd round –
This is a interactive round that the data interpretation questions will be displayed on the screen. You have to guess the puzzles which is displayed and who finds it can give the answer.
3rd round –
The last round is logical reasoning. A volunteer is selected from each team who comes first and makes the balloon burst can give the answer.
